# PAC-MAN's Game - Horror Edition

A 3D web-based horror-themed PAC-MAN game where the tables are turned. In this nightmare maze, **PAC-MAN is the predator**, and your mission is to **save the ghosts** from being devoured.

## Gameplay

- **You are NOT PAC-MAN** - You're a survivor trapped in the maze with the relentless yellow hunter
- **First-person perspective** - Navigate the eerie corridors with your flashlight, seeing your hands and feet
- **Save the ghosts** - Click near ghosts to guide them to the glowing green safe zones before PAC-MAN catches them
- **PAC-MAN plays automatically** - He roams the maze, eating dots and hunting ghosts just like the classic game
- **Power Pellets = DANGER** - When PAC-MAN eats a power pellet, he becomes even more aggressive!

## Controls

| Action | Control |
|--------|---------|
| Move | WASD |
| Look | Mouse |
| Run | Hold Shift |
| Guide Ghost | Left Click (when near a ghost) |

## How to Play

1. Install dependencies: `npm install`
2. Run the development server: `npm run dev`
3. Open your browser to the local server URL
4. Click "ENTER THE MAZE" and click to lock your mouse
5. Navigate to the ghosts and click when nearby to guide them to safety (green zones)
6. Save all 4 ghosts before PAC-MAN devours them!

## Win Condition

Guide all 4 ghosts to the safe zones (glowing green areas) before PAC-MAN catches them all.

## Game Over

The game ends when PAC-MAN has eaten all remaining ghosts that haven't been saved.
